My embassadours

Artist embassadours is the fans that are the most imporant for your career. An such fan has helped you a lot with sharing your music. When you add that follower (fan) as an embassador, he / she will be shown here under your profile as an embassador. An embassador works like a freelancher AnR who are really loving your music and promoting it by listening, sharing and making the most of your music. In exchange for their good thing, you can reward your embassadors at any time by sending them an secret link to a pre-release or similar
